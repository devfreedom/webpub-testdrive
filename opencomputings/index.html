Welcome to the world of open source.

In computing, the term "open source" refers to any software or hardware which its source code or design is made available to the public.

Also "free software" means open source software with a wide range of permission to use, distribute, or modify; More emphasis on the freedom itself.

From messaging apps to DIY security camera, Opencomputings is dedicated to provide comprehensive and practical information on free/open computing systems that everyone can make use of.

Because you deserve better options.

What's so special about open source?

Open source is a way to share passion and achievement in engineering, and promote businesses in a reciprocal way.

This makes most of open source products to be free of charge and available to everyone.

Open source means everyone can see what it's made of and how it works.

Because of this, there are lesser reason to take advantage of you. It's easier to spot security issues and fix it as well.

Open source allows people to freely use, improve, contribute, and create something new in various ways.

Imagine what's possible when collective intelligence meets freedom. Imagine what you can do with it.
